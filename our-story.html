The Slate Milk Story

Great ideas often come from unlikely places. For Slate Milk, it all started with a simple craving: the nostalgic, comforting taste of chocolate milk—but without the sugar crash or stomach aches.

Where It All Started

The story of Slate Milk begins on a college campus, where two lifelong friends, Manny and Josh, realized that their favorite childhood drink—chocolate milk—wasn’t made for adults. While they still loved the flavor, they were frustrated by the high sugar content, poor nutritional value, and digestive discomfort caused by lactose.

That’s when the lightbulb moment happened. What if there was a better version of chocolate milk—one that had all the taste but none of the drawbacks?

Founders with a Mission

Driven by their shared passion for entrepreneurship, health, and flavor, Manny and Josh made it their mission to reinvent chocolate milk for the modern consumer. They envisioned a product that would meet the nutritional needs of athletes, professionals, and busy adults while still delivering that rich, creamy, satisfying chocolate taste.

They didn’t want to compromise—and they didn’t want anyone else to either.

The Problem with Traditional Chocolate Milk

Most chocolate milks on the market are made with added sugars, preservatives, and lactose—a sugar that many adults have trouble digesting. These drinks are often marketed to kids and fall short of providing any real nutritional benefits.

That just didn’t sit right with the founders of Slate. Why shouldn’t chocolate milk grow up, just like the rest of us?

Creating a Modern, Healthier Milk

Manny and Josh got to work, consulting with food scientists, nutritionists, and dairy experts to create a new kind of milk. After months of research and testing, they landed on the formula that would become the cornerstone of Slate Milk:

  • Lactose-free
  • 20g of protein per can
  • 0g added sugar
  • Shelf-stable and portable

Using ultra-filtration and natural sweeteners, they created a milk that’s easy on the stomach, high in protein, and incredibly delicious.

The Journey to Launch

Launching a beverage brand from scratch wasn’t easy. There were challenges at every turn—from perfecting the formula, to designing eco-friendly cans, to building a community of early supporters. But in 2019, Slate Milk launched with a successful Kickstarter campaign that proved one thing:

People were ready for a better kind of chocolate milk.

Since then, Slate has grown from a startup idea to a nationwide brand available in major retailers and online. From fitness enthusiasts to chocolate lovers, Slate has earned a loyal following that’s only growing.

Our Commitment Today

At Slate, we believe that great taste and great nutrition can go hand in hand. Our mission is to provide:

  • Clean, functional beverages
  • Delicious flavor with simple ingredients
  • Options for lactose-intolerant and health-conscious individuals

We’re constantly innovating, expanding our flavors, and exploring new ways to help people fuel their day with something they actually enjoy drinking.

But beyond the product, we’re proud of the community we’ve built—a community that supports better choices, smarter nutrition, and honest ingredients.

Join the Slate Movement

Whether you’re here for the protein, the flavor, or the nostalgia, we’re glad you found us. Slate Milk isn’t just a drink—it’s a movement. A movement to change how people think about chocolate milk. A movement to fuel ambition without compromise.

So crack open a can, take a sip, and taste the difference. Let’s make chocolate milk something we can all be proud of again.
