High-Protein Recipes Using Slate Milk

Whether you’re powering up for a workout, looking for a mid-day pick-me-up, or just want something deliciously nutritious, Slate Milk is your go-to base for easy, high-protein recipes. Our ultra-filtered, lactose-free chocolate milk is more than just a drink—it’s a versatile ingredient that levels up your health game.

Let’s dive into some creative, protein-packed recipes you can make with your favorite Slate flavors.

Chocolate Milk Recipe Ideas

There's something nostalgic and comforting about chocolate milk—but with Slate, it’s leveled up with protein and clean ingredients. Try a Chocolate Banana Recovery Shake by blending:

  • 1 can Slate Classic Chocolate
  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1 tbsp peanut butter
  • 1 scoop chocolate protein powder
  • Handful of ice

This smoothie clocks in at over 35g of protein and is perfect post-workout or as a meal replacement.

Protein-Packed Smoothies

Smoothies are the fastest way to get nutrients and protein in your day. Thanks to Slate Milk’s creamy texture and sweet-but-clean taste, it blends seamlessly into nearly every smoothie. Try the Blueberry Power Smoothie:

  • 1 can Slate French Vanilla
  • ½ cup frozen blueberries
  • ½ cup Greek yogurt
  • 1 scoop vanilla protein
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds

Blend until smooth. Great for breakfast or pre-workout fuel!

Post-Workout Protein Shakes

After a heavy lift session or cardio run, your body craves recovery fuel. Enter the Mocha Muscle Shake:

  • 1 can Slate Mocha Latte
  • 1 shot espresso (optional)
  • 1 scoop chocolate whey or vegan protein
  • Ice

This shake delivers a caffeine kick and muscle-building power in under 2 minutes.

Hot Chocolate with Protein

Nothing hits like a cozy hot chocolate after a winter workout or during a study break. Instead of sugary powders, try heating up a can of Slate Classic Chocolate, then whisk in:

  • 1 scoop collagen or unflavored protein powder
  • Dash of cinnamon or cayenne
  • Mini marshmallows if you’re feeling festive

Boom—comfort meets function.

Overnight Oats with Slate Milk

Overnight oats are great for meal prep, and Slate Milk makes them taste like dessert. Try Chocolate Protein Overnight Oats:

  • ½ cup oats
  • 1 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 1 scoop chocolate protein
  • ¾ can Slate Classic Chocolate
  • Toppings: peanut butter, berries, dark chocolate chips

Combine all ingredients in a jar and refrigerate overnight. Grab and go in the morning!

Mocha and Coffee-Based Drinks

Craving that iced mocha vibe but want to skip the sugar bomb? Mix up this Iced Mocha Protein Latte:

  • 1 can Slate Mocha Latte (cold)
  • 1 scoop chocolate or mocha protein
  • Shake in a blender bottle with ice
  • Pour over ice and top with oat milk or whipped cream

It’s energizing, filling, and far healthier than coffee shop versions.

Bonus Ideas

  • Protein Popsicles using Slate + Greek yogurt
  • Baked protein brownies with Slate instead of milk
  • High-protein chia pudding with Slate Vanilla
  • Protein pancakes using Slate as a milk substitute

Why Use Slate Milk in Recipes?

Slate isn’t just lactose-free and ultra-filtered. It’s a smart choice for recipes because:

  • It adds 20g of protein per can
  • It's shelf-stable and travel-ready
  • It has no added sugar (depending on flavor)
  • It blends better than traditional milk or powders

Plus, it tastes great—like the chocolate milk you loved as a kid, with grown-up nutrition.
