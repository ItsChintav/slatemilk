Our Commitment to Sustainability

Eco-Friendly Practices for a Better Tomorrow

Our Commitment to Sustainability

At Slate Milk, we believe delicious drinks shouldn’t come at the planet’s expense. That’s why sustainability is at the core of everything we do—from the farms we partner with to the packaging we use. We’re on a mission to change the way people think about chocolate milk. But we’re also here to change the impact it has on the environment.

Why Sustainability Matters to Us

We didn’t start Slate just to create better chocolate milk—we wanted to build a better future for the beverage industry. As passionate lovers of health, nutrition, and the outdoors, we knew we had to take responsibility for our impact on the planet. Every decision we make—from how we source our milk to how we ship our products—is guided by our commitment to the environment and the people who live in it.

Eco-Friendly Packaging

One of the biggest ways we reduce waste is through 100% recyclable aluminum cans. Unlike plastic, which can take centuries to decompose and often ends up in landfills or oceans, aluminum is one of the most sustainable packaging materials on the planet. It’s:

  • Infinitely recyclable
  • Lightweight (reducing shipping emissions)
  • More efficient to recycle than glass or plastic

Every can of Slate Milk you drink can be melted down and made into something new—again and again.

Responsible Dairy Sourcing

Sustainability starts with our supply chain. We partner with family-owned dairy farms that are committed to:

  • Ethical treatment of cows
  • Reducing methane emissions
  • Water conservation
  • Sustainable feed practices

Our milk comes from farms that share our values—caring for animals, the land, and the long-term health of our food system. Plus, we use ultra-filtration technology to reduce waste and maximize the nutritional value of every drop.

Reducing Our Carbon Footprint

We’re not perfect, but we’re working every day to lower our carbon impact. Some of our ongoing efforts include:

  • Optimized shipping logistics to reduce fuel use
  • Shelf-stable products, which require no refrigeration until opened
  • Bulk ordering options to reduce packaging waste
  • Minimalist design to cut down on ink, plastic wrap, and printing waste

Sustainability isn't about a single solution—it's about a thousand smart choices that add up to real change.

Transparency and Accountability

We believe that sustainability should be more than just a buzzword. That’s why we’re transparent about our practices and continually look for ways to improve. We actively seek feedback from customers, partners, and environmental experts. Our team regularly reviews our practices to ensure we’re:

  • Meeting industry sustainability standards
  • Working toward measurable goals
  • Innovating to reduce waste and improve energy use

We’re not afraid to be held accountable—because accountability drives real progress.

Looking Ahead

Sustainability isn’t a destination. It’s a journey. And at Slate Milk, we’re just getting started. Our future goals include:

  • Partnering with carbon offset programs
  • Exploring compostable packaging alternatives
  • Collaborating with climate-focused organizations
  • Educating our customers on how to recycle and reduce waste

Together, we can build a better future for chocolate milk—and the world.

Join Us on the Journey

Every time you choose Slate, you’re not just choosing a delicious, high-protein chocolate milk. You’re supporting a brand that puts the planet first. Small choices make a big difference. And we’re proud to have you with us.
