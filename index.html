No Lactose, No Sugar – All Flavor

If you’ve been avoiding milk due to lactose intolerance or concerns about sugar, you no longer have to compromise. Slate Milk is 100% lactose-free, so it’s easy on your stomach and still packed with that rich, chocolatey taste you remember.
